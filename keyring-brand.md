## Voice & Personality
- **Keywords:** Trustworthy, Clean, Modern, Calm, Capable, Organized
- **Avoid:** Alarming, Fear-based, Technical jargon, Paranoid, Corporate, Heavy-handed
- **Tone:** KeyRing speaks like a calm, capable colleague who has everything organized. Not a compliance tool. Not a password nagger. It reassures without dramatizing. Security is the foundation, not the personality. The app feels like it just works — clean, fast, in control.
- **Positioning:** KeyRing is the vault that professionals actually want to use. Free, forever. Built by Realmdrop for designers, developers, and freelancers who manage credentials and brand assets for clients.

## Notes
KeyRing is a free product by Realmdrop Software. It inherits the full Realmdrop color system — Deep Violet & Mint — with no additional brand colors introduced. Target audience: designers, developers, freelancers, and agencies who manage secrets and brand kits across multiple client projects. A future KeyRing Cloud tier will introduce encrypted cloud sync and team access — the brand should be able to grow into that without a redesign. The mono font (JetBrains Mono) is unique to KeyRing within the Realmdrop family — it signals the developer/technical credential context inside the app UI.
